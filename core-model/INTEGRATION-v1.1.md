# MBP Integration

**6-Phase Workflow**

---

## Overview

```
Phase 0: Safety → Phase 1: Core → Phase 2: Probing → Phase 3: Mining → Phase 4: Validation → Phase 5: Synthesis → Phase 6: Closure
```

---

## Phase 0: Safety & Context

**Tujuan:** Establish readiness & safety container

- Readiness check (stabilitas, support system)
- Informed consent (stress testing disclosure)
- Cultural context calibration
- Safe word / pause mechanism

---

## Phase 1: Core Questions

**Tujuan:** Build baseline & initial hypothesis

| Section | Fokus | Mengisi |
|---------|-------|---------|
| **A. Identity & Origin** | Lingkungan, relasi orang tua, nilai | CFV, ARP, RS, DMC |
| **B. Projection & Conflict** | Sifat dibenci, cara hadapi konflik | DMC, RSI, RS, COI |
| **C. Regret & Decision** | Keputusan disesali, proses decide | CDI, CRF, COI, CFV |
| **D. Vitality & Energy** | Flow, energi, passion | ASC, Core Drive, AB |

**Probes:**
- Inconsistency: "Apa bagian dirimu yang bertentangan dengan gambaran ini?"
- Temporal: "Bandingkan dirimu 5 tahun lalu vs sekarang"
- Specificity: "Ceritakan satu momen konkret..."

---

## Phase 2: Adaptive Probing

**Tujuan:** Fill gaps & test hypotheses

**Trigger:**
- If confidence < 0.7 → Probe deeper
- If contradiction detected → Devil's advocate
- If unclear pattern → Forced choice

**Teknik:**
- **Devil's Advocate:** Cari bukti kontra untuk setiap hipotesis
- **Forced Choice:** Trade-off scenarios ("diabaikan teman vs dikritik publik")
- **Surprise Probe:** "Apa yang paling sering disalahpahami orang tentang kamu?"

---

## Phase 3: Adaptation Pattern Mining

**Tujuan:** Identify survival patterns

**Bukan:** "Apa traumamu?"  
**Tapi:** "Apa aturan tak tertulis yang kamu pelajari untuk survive?"

**Probes:**
- Somatic: "Kapan tubuhmu bereaksi sebelum pikiranmu proses?"
- Attachment: "Bagaimana perasaanmu saat membicarakan masa kecil?"
- Rule: "Apa aturan untuk survive di keluargamu?"

---

## Phase 4: Structural Cross-Validation

**Tujuan:** Verify consistency & explore contradictions

**Validasi:**
- Projection vs Regret
- Conflict style vs Authority response
- Emotional granularity vs Vulnerability
- Core fear vs Control orientation

**Contradiction Handling:**  
Jangan hapus — eksplorasi: "Apakah ada dua 'bagian' berbeda dalam dirimu?"

---

## Phase 5: Synthesis

**Tujuan:** Generate Matrix profile

**Output:**
- 12 sub-dimensi scores dengan confidence intervals
- Key interaction patterns
- Growth vectors
- Assessment quality rating

---

## Phase 6: Debriefing & Closure

**Tujuan:** Safe landing

- Grounding: "Kembali ke sekarang — apa yang kamu lihat?"
- Resource: "Apa yang membuatmu merasa aman/kuat?"
- Closure: "Proses selesai. Hasil dianalisis, bukan diagnosa."

---

## Data Architecture

```
Layer 3: Synthesis (Matrix Profile, confidence intervals)
Layer 2: Signals (Tagged responses, hypothesis registry, contradiction log)
Layer 1: Raw (Transcript, metadata, engagement quality)
```

---

## Prinsip Kritis

1. **Safety First** — Phase 0 & 6 bukan optional
2. **Contradiction is Data** — Eksplorasi, bukan hapus
3. **Confidence-Aware** — Interval, bukan absolut
4. **Adaptation > Trauma** — Cara bertahan, bukan luka
5. **Iterative** — Re-assess, track evolution
