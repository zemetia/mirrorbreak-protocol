# What is the MirrorBreak Protocol (MBP)?

The MirrorBreak Protocol (MBP) is a **qualitative structural analysis framework**. Its primary goal is to understand human behavior by looking at how people adapt to their environments over time.

## What it IS:
*   **A map of adaptations**: It looks at how you learned to survive and thrive in challenging emotional or relational environments.
*   **A tool for deep understanding**: It explores the gap between the "persona" you naturally show the world and your hidden "core" self.
*   **Context-aware**: It heavily considers your cultural background and personal history.

## What it IS NOT:
*   **Not a personality test**: MBP doesn't label you or fit you into rigid boxes like MBTI or the Big Five. There are no "types" here.
*   **Not a clinical diagnosis**: It doesn't diagnose mental health conditions. It is meant for personal development, not medical treatment.
*   **Not about "right" or "wrong"**: Every trait you have is viewed as a necessary adaptation to a specific pressure. There are no bad traits, only learned survival strategies.
