## Phase 3: Validation (6-18 months)

### Priority 3.1: Empirical Validation Roadmap

| Fase | Aktivitas | Timeline | Success Criteria |
|------|-----------|----------|------------------|
| Pilot | Test 50+ subjects, refine rubrics | 3-6 bulan | Rubric stability >0.8 |
