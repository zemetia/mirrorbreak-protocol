# The 6 Phases of MBP Analysis

When someone goes through the MirrorBreak Protocol (either with a human analyst or an AI), they are guided through a carefully structured 6-phase journey. 

This process is designed to bypass surface-level answers and discover exactly how a person's emotional structure operates.

### Phase 0: Safety First
Before anything begins, a critical safety check is performed. We ensure the person is in a stable place, ready for deep reflection, and fully understands they can pause or stop the session at any time.

### Phase 1: Core Questioning
We start with fixed, foundational questions to build a baseline. We look at your original family environment, how you handle daily conflict, what decisions you regret, and what activities naturally bring you energy.

### Phase 2: Adaptive Probing
Based on your early answers, the analyst forms hypotheses about your survival mechanisms. We use specialized, targeted questions to test these ideas. We never ask direct, obvious questions (like "Are you a perfectionist?"); instead, we ask about specific memories and moments in your life to see how you naturally reacted.

### Phase 3: Pattern Mining
This phase digs deep into the origins of your behaviors. We do not focus on traumatic details; instead, we focus on the **"unwritten rules"** you learned in order to survive your early environments. 

### Phase 4: Structural Cross-Validation
Here, we look for contradictions. If you claim to be highly analytical but your body freezes under emotional stress, we gently explore that gap. We expose the tension between what your mind thinks and what your body does.

### Phase 5 & 6: Synthesis and Closure
We put all the pieces together into a complete structural profile. Finally, we ensure a "safe landing," grounding you back in the present moment. You leave the session with out judgment, but with a clear roadmap for turning your deepest adaptations into your greatest strengths.
